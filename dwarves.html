Overview

The Dwarves are a proud and resilient race, master craftsmen and unmatched miners. Created by Aulë, they are known for their honor, loyalty, and a fierce determination to reclaim what is theirs. Though short in stature, their courage and endurance are legendary.

Major Homes

  • Erebor (The Lonely Mountain) – The greatest Dwarven kingdom in the later Third Age
  • Khazad-dûm (Moria) – Ancient and vast realm, later overrun by a Balrog
  • Iron Hills – Stronghold of Dáin Ironfoot’s forces
  • Blue Mountains – Western Dwarf colonies near the Shire
  • Glittering Caves (Aglarond) – Beautiful caverns near Helm’s Deep, gifted to the Dwarves by King Elessar

Major Battles

  • Battle of Azanulbizar – Dwarves fight Orcs at the gates of Moria; great loss and eventual victory
  • Battle of the Five Armies – Thorin’s company, Iron Hills Dwarves, Elves, and Men defend Erebor from goblins and wargs
  • Siege of Erebor – Thorin Oakenshield defends Erebor against Men and Elves before the arrival of the Orc army
  • Fall of Moria – Though not shown directly, the battle against the Balrog and Orcs leads to the loss of Khazad-dûm
